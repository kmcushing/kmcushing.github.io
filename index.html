Research

January 2024 - Present

I am currently working with the C3 Lab on a system for automatically generating datasets consisting of natural language questions and corresponding SQL queries from databases covering a wide variety domains. Our approach aims to enhance text-to-SQL models by facilitating the creation of robust in-domain datasets, allowing these models to more accurately provide data-driven answers to questions posed by people without knowledge of SQL and database design.
